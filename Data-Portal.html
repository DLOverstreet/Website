About the Census Portal

A powerful tool for exploring and analyzing comprehensive census data.

The Alt-30 Census Portal provides access to detailed demographic, economic, and social data from the latest census. Our interactive platform makes it easy to explore trends, compare regions, and extract meaningful insights.

Interactive Visualizations

Explore data through intuitive charts, maps, and graphs that respond to your inputs in real-time.

Advanced Filtering

Narrow down data by demographics, geographic locations, time periods, and specific metrics to find exactly what you need.

Visualize data across different regions with color-coded maps that highlight patterns and trends.

Download your findings as reports, images, or raw data for use in your own analyses and presentations.
